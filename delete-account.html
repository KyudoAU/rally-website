Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to permanently delete your Rally account and all associated data.

Delete from the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly in the Rally app:

  1. Open the Rally app and sign in
  2. Go to Settings (tap the gear icon)
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted

⚠️ This action is permanent. Deleting your account will immediately remove your profile, any circles you've created, and all associated data. This cannot be undone.

Request Deletion via Email

If you can't access the app or need assistance, you can request account deletion by email:

📧 Contact Support

Email hello@rally-around.us with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your Rally account.

We'll process your request within 48 hours and send confirmation once your account has been deleted.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  1. Your profile and email address
  2. Any care circles you created
  3. All tasks you created or claimed
  4. Your membership in other people's circles
