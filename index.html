How it works

Set up in minutes.
Coordinate with ease.

No training required. No complex setup. Just a simple, structured way to bring people together.

1

Create a Circle

The Organiser sets up a Rally for the person who needs support — a surgery recovery, illness, new baby, or anything life throws your way.

2

Add tasks

Break down what's needed into specific tasks — meals, lifts, errands, school pickups. Set dates, times, and details so helpers know exactly what to do.

3

Invite helpers

Share a simple invite link. Friends, family, and neighbours join the circle and claim the tasks they can help with.

4

Everyone's covered

Helpers see their upcoming tasks. Organisers see the full picture. No one falls through the cracks.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for the moments when you need to focus on the people who matter — not logistics.

📋

Task management

Create tasks with types (meal, transport, errand), scheduled times, locations, and notes. Helpers claim what they can do — no double-bookings, no confusion.

🔔

Smart notifications

Helpers get reminded about upcoming tasks. Organisers are notified when tasks are claimed or completed. Always know the status without chasing.

📅

Google Calendar sync

Helpers can sync claimed tasks directly to their Google Calendar so nothing slips through the cracks.

🔗

Simple sharing

Invite helpers via a single shareable link. No app required to receive the invite — joining takes seconds.

🛡️

Privacy first

Helpers only see their tasks and the circle they've joined. Personal details are protected. No data sold, ever.

✏️

Full organiser control

Edit tasks, update the circle details, and manage the full picture from a clean dashboard. Organisers stay in control.
